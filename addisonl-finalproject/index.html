MonoMuse

Alma Thomas (1891–1978) was a pioneering abstract expressionist known for her vibrant, mosaic-like paintings inspired by nature and space. Her joyful use of color broke new ground for African American women in the art world.

Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary art museum dedicated to showcasing the full spectrum of creative expression. From paintings and sculpture to digital media and immersive installations, our rotating exhibitions feature emerging and established artists from around the world. Whether you are a seasoned collector or stepping into a gallery for the first time, MonoMuse offers experiences that inspire curiosity, spark conversation, and connect communities.
