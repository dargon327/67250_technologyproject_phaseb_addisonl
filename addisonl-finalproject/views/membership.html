Become a MonoMuse Member

Join a community of art lovers and get closer to the work that inspires you. MonoMuse members enjoy year-round benefits that make every visit more rewarding, from discounted admission and exclusive previews to member-only events.

Member Benefits

  • Early access to new exhibitions before public opening
  • Invitations to member-only events and artist talks
  • 10% discount at the MonoMuse Shop and Café
  • 10% discount for admission for general members
  • Monthly newsletter with behind-the-scenes content

Membership Tiers

Student

$20 / year

Perfect for full-time students. Enjoy discounted admission, exhibition previews, and student-exclusive workshops. Valid student ID required.

Regular

$50 / year

Regular membership. Includes all core benefits plus priority registration for special events.
