Exhibitions

Exhibition 1: Francis Bacon

Francis Bacon (1909-1992) was an Irish-born British figurative painter known for his raw, emotionally charged imagery. His work often depicted the human body in states of distortion and anguish, drawing on themes of isolation, mortality, and the human condition.

Exhibition 2: Andy Warhol

Andy Warhol (1928-1987) was an American artist, filmmaker, and leading figure of the Pop Art movement. Born and raised in Pittsburgh, Warhol transformed everyday consumer objects (Campbell's Soup Cans, Coca-Cola bottles, and celebrity portraits) into fine art, blurring the line between commercial and high culture.
