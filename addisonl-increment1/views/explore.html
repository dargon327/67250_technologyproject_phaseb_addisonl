MonoMuse is committed to connecting visitors with modern art, interactive media, and emerging technology through memorable and inclusive museum experiences.

Our museum serves students, artists, families, researchers, and curious visitors who want to learn how creativity and innovation shape the world around us.

  • Mission: To inspire creativity through the fusion of art and technology.
  • Founded: 2018
  • Who We Serve: Local communities, students, tourists, and digital art enthusiasts.
  • Milestone: Opened the first interactive AI art gallery in 2020.
  • Milestone: Expanded educational workshops and school programs in 2023.
